Asia’s FIRST and ONLY Startup Accelerator for Sports Innovation

Asia’s FIRST and ONLY Startup Accelerator for Sports Innovation

SPIN Accelerator Taiwan is designed to accelerate startups looking to gain traction in the Taiwanese and global sports industry.

Startups focusing on sports-tech, sports-service, sports-medicine, sports-entertainment or any sports-related industry, regardless of your developing stage, are all welcome to apply.

Tailored to You

IAPS will tailor the program to each startup’s needs to help you pursue strategic opportunities for industry connections, networking, and investment rounds.

Industry Leaders

IAPS has invited a cast of all-stars from the sports tech industry to share their personal experiences.

Global Network

Startups can hone their international edge through English pitch + story-telling prep, one-on-one coaching with experienced entrepreneurs, and online masterclasses with overseas experts from the global HYPE community.

Final Demo Day

One Team from each phase will earn the chance to represent Asia at HYPE’s Global Pitch Competitions, as well as free office residency at Taiwan Tech Arena.

About IAPS

As the first university-based accelerator in Taiwan, IAPS connects startups, corporates, mentors and overseas partners to a comprehensive startup ecosystem. Since 2013, we’ve accelerated over 500 startups and raised 2-3 million USD annually. We provide personalized mentor-ship, matchmaking events and global connections for business development.

About HYPE Sports Innovation

HYPE Sports Innovation is the largest global ecosystem for sports innovation with more than 29,000 key industry members. HYPE’s Global Sports Innovation Community includes:

  • 2,900 Global Sports & Tech Brand Leaders
  • 700 Investors & VCs
  • 450 Clubs
  • More than 9,000 Sports Startups
  • Over 29,000 Members Globally
