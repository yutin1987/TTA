Get Immersed Program - Q2 2018
2018.08.01

Taiwan Tech Arena is happy to announce the launch of its “Get Immersed” program. This is a curated one-month program for early to scale-up stage startups to come to Taiwan Tech Arena and gain access to our network of talents, corporate partners, global connections, investors and marketing platform. Our goals are to work closely with the best startups around the world and to help them go big along with the deep tech ecosystem and industry in Taiwan.

We welcome and encourage startups in deep technology, specifically using AI or advanced technology in IoT, ICT, biotechnology, healthcare, fintech/insurance tech, etc. to apply. Startups should have a proof of concept ready and are required to fill in a simple form here to complete the application. The Taiwan Tech Arena team will work with a core group of corporate partner representatives to jointly review the application. Applicants should expect a response within 2 weeks after submitting their application. Video calls may be scheduled to learn more about the applicants. Our main criteria will be the motivation of applicants and if the technology/business of the applicants is a good match for Taiwan’s deep tech ecosystem.

Please refer to the attachment for the program details:

Invitation to Apply for Taiwan Tech Arena

Once an applicant is accepted, we will assign dedicated account managers to work closely with the startups to understand the resources they wish to receive from the program, and to arrange meetings and participation at events before they arrive in Taiwan.

APPLY NOW

***

Important: When answering “how did you hear about this program?” in the application form, you can enter “tta-website” if you saw the news here. Or, if you were referred by the Taiwan Tech Arena team or any other people, please fill in “tta-name of the people-org”.

Application Timeline:

For September batch, the application deadline will be at 23:59, August 31, 2018 (GMT+8).

Program Contact:

If there is any question, please feel free to let us know by writing to contact@titan.org.tw. Look forward to hearing from you!
