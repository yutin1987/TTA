Accelerator 2018.07.27
MOX - Mobile Only Accelerator
2018.07.27

MOX — the Mobile Only Accelerator — helps the best mobile startups from around the world acquire millions of users in Southeast Asia, India, Eastern Europe and South America. MOX brings free user acquisition to the 167 million users on its smartphone platform and investment in return for revenue share and equity. The strategic partnership kicks off with a two-month accelerator program where MOX provides 50-150k users to localize the app and optimize retention and monetization market by market. The first global accelerator in Taiwan, MOX is operated by SOSV – the Accelerator VC – a venture fund with $300M assets under management investing through seven accelerators in startups from around the world by industry vertical in Internet, Hardware, Biotech and Food.
