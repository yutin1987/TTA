SparkLabs Taipei startup application starts now!

SparkLabs Taipei helps startups expand their business internationally by working with successful entrepreneurs, multi-national companies, and international investors.

Selected startups will receive USD 40,000 funding in exchange for up to 6 percent in equity. SparkLabs also provides office space, training courses, and access to SparkLabs’ network of 200+ mentors in Silicon Valley, London, Korea, Japan, China, Hong Kong, Australia, and Taiwan.
