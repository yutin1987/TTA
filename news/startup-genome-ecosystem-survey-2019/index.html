TTA cooperates with Startup Genome to put Taiwan on the global map of innovation
2018.12.20

Founders and executives: we need your help to put the Taiwan startup ecosystem on the global map of innovation – to attract more talent and money to it.

How? Just take 15 minutes to complete the Global Startup Ecosystem survey.

Your input is crucial.

In answering this survey, you’ll help us understand and serve your needs better and become an important actor to enhance startup success rate, attract investors and scale up. Let’s achieve a 100% participation rate!

 

*All the information that you share in the survey is completely confidential.

*It will be used only in aggregate for the research and it will never be given or sold to third parties.

 

 

About Startup Genome

Since 2011, Startup Genome has been working on identifying gaps and assessing startup ecosystems around the world.

Over the last 7 years, Startup Genome has provided a way for entrepreneurs everywhere to tell their journeys and their regions – giving their local expertise a voice at the policy-making table. The primary research with founders, supplemented with secondary research and data from global and local partners, helps create the world’s most comprehensive research on startups.

Fueled by primary research with 10,000+ startups annually, Startup Genome aims to support local ecosystem leaders building consensus for action on key challenges and generate reports which assist in attracting more investors and top talent in different regions.
